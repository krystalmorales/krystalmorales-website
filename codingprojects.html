Finding Wildlife

In collaboration with Shreya Menon and Mason Spalding, we developed "Finding Wildlife," a dynamic web application designed for nature enthusiasts and US National Park explorers. Our platform empowers users to create accounts, share trail and park reviews, and document animal sightings with precise location pinning using an integrated map API.
