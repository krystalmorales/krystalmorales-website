Over the past decade, I've had the incredible opportunity to explore the world, immersing myself in diverse cultures and connecting with people from all walks of life. My passion for learning and discovery led me to study abroad in Geneva and Mexico City during my undergraduate years, and later, a Tinker Field Research grant allowed me to conduct research in Peru during the summer of 2019.

Eager to apply my academic knowledge and skills in a practical setting, I joined Genesis10 where I earned a full-stack Java developer certificate. This experience provided me with a solid foundation in software development and further ignited my interest in the field.

To deepen my expertise, I pursued a post-baccalaureate degree in computer science at Tufts University, which I completed in 2024. I'm now excited to leverage my diverse experiences and technical skills to contribute to the tech indistry.
